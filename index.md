### About me
I received the B.S. degree from Qingdao University of Science and Technology, in 2019. I am currently a master student at Sichuan University. My main research interests include **cell-ECM mechanobiology**, **cell polarization**, **cancer cell** and **biomaterials**. Also, I have conducted some light research on **computer vision** and **cell mechanics models**, such as analyzing fluorescent images of the cytoskeleton, focusing on solving scientific problems related to cell mechanics.
